Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba is a popular Japanese manga and anime series by Koyoharu Gotouge. The story follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy whose family is slaughtered by demons, with his sister Nezuko surviving but turned into a demon. Determined to avenge his family and cure Nezuko, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, a group of skilled warriors trained to fight demons. The series showcases intense battles, unique breathing techniques, and rich character development. It is praised for its stunning animation, emotional depth, and epic storytelling.
