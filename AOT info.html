Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin) is a renowned Japanese manga and anime series created by Hajime Isayama. The story is set in a world where humanity lives behind massive walls to protect themselves from man-eating giants called Titans. It follows Eren Yeager, who vows to eliminate all Titans after witnessing the destruction of his home and his mother's death. As Eren joins the military, the series uncovers deeper mysteries about the Titans, the origins of the walls, and humanity's survival. Known for its intense action, complex characters, and shocking twists, Attack on Titan explores themes of freedom, morality, and the consequences of war.
